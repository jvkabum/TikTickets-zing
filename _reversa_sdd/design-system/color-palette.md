| `$primary` | `#5c6bc0` | Cor primária da marca (Indigo) |

## Gradientes e Glassmorphism
| Classe | Valor CSS Principal | Efeito |
|--------|---------------------|--------|
| `.bg-padrao` | `linear-gradient(135deg, $primary, $accent)` | Padrão Tema |
| `.grad-primary` | `linear-gradient(135deg, #6366f1 0%, #a855f7 100%)` | Premium Gradient |
| `.grad-success` | `linear-gradient(135deg, #22c55e 0%, #10b981 100%)` | Verde Premium |
| `.grad-warning` | `linear-gradient(135deg, #f59e0b 0%, #ef4444 100%)` | Laranja/Vermelho |
| `.grad-info` | `linear-gradient(135deg, #0ea5e9 0%, #2563eb 100%)` | Azul Tech |
| `.grad-error` | `linear-gradient(135deg, #ef4444 0%, #991b1b 100%)` | Vermelho Intenso |
